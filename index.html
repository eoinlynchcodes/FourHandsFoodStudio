ABOUT
4 Hands is the creation of duo Rose Greene and Margaux Dejardin, both highly knowledgeable in food, nutrition, farming and health, developed their business to fit their lifestyle and ethos. Coming from a background in world renowned kitchens Rose came to a point where she needed a change; mentally, physically and emotionally. Margaux forged a life in food later in life, both determined to create their own path in life. After taking time to think, share, learn and really determine what role they wanted to play and where they wanted to establish their business, Ireland became the natural choice, it happened almost accidently, but has proven the natural place to be. Starting small was always the way they wanted to build 4 Hands, allowing growth at a sustainable pace for both themselves whilst supporting the natural progression of a healthy business.
