The want to engage with and educate people was the driving force for the creation of 4 Hands, Rose and Margaux have gone through a time of change in order to create a more balanced and grounded lifestyle for themselves, while doing what they truly believe in. Rose and Margaux wish to share their knowledge and help others to introduce beneficial changes to their diet, lifestyle and daily habits, not only for their wellbeing but for the health of our environment and all it supports. To be able to source and make nutritious food for yourself and the people you care for is the most fundamentally important skill in life, 4 Hands tailor workshops to share skills in an open and natural environment, allowing for exchange and empowerment.
